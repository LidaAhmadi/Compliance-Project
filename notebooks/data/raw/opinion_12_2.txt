<p>DECISION &amp; ORDER</p>
<p>Application by the defendant for a writ of error coram nobis seeking leave to file a late notice of appeal from a judgment of the Supreme Court, Queens County (Karen Gopee, J.), rendered February 16, 2022.</p>
<p>ORDERED that the application is granted, and the defendant's application is deemed to be a timely notice of appeal.</p>
<p>The defendant has established his entitlement to the relief requested (<i>see People v Syville</i>, <span class="citation" data-id="5486244"><a href="/opinion/5640527/people-v-syville/" aria-description="Citation for case: People v. Syville">15 NY3d 391</a></span>).</p>
<p>CONNOLLY, J.P., MILLER, VENTURA and MCCORMACK, JJ., concur.</p>
